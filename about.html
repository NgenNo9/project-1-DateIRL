About Us

Think about it, every time you’re on a date with someone you’ve met through a dating app, one of the first questions is “How has your insert-dating-app-name-here journey been going?”. Invariably, the answer is always something along the lines of “Terrible”.

Typically these apps tend to go with one date, maybe 2 and then that’s it. Ghosted. Or even worse, the person turned out not to be who they seemed from behind that keyboard.

This is where we want to make a difference. We want to bring people together so that they don’t have to find out whether a person is better or different behind a keyboard. Get comfortable knowing each other truly, right from the get go... IN REAL LIFE.

Finding the one can mean a lifetime of happiness or of indifference. Kurt Vonnegut once said “A purpose of human life... is to love whoever is around to be loved.” Wouldn’t it be best that you have a significant other to be that one to love.... or be loved.
